National Coloured Shirt Program

Purpose

The Coloured Shirt Program seeks to promote basic umpire training and development. It is designed for those who are new to the role of umpiring.

The aim is to teach the fundamentals of umpiring, enhancing the development of umpires and providing a smooth link to the Level 1 Introduction to Umpiring Program.

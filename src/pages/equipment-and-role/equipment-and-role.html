Equipment and Role

Plate umpires stand behind the catcher, call balls and strikes, and control many aspects of the game. They need to wear protective equipment, including

  • a face mask
  • a chest protector
  • leg guards
  • suitable footwear

Base umpires start near the bases, and are responsible for making safe and out calls at the bases. They also need appropriate footwear.

All umpires should carry a ball/strike/out counter ("clicker"), which they operate with their left hand.

Beginner umpire

Beginner umpires often worry about technical rules such as interference or obstruction. While these are important elements of the game, it is important to stress that beginners are not expected to know all the rules immediately. Rule knowledge builds naturally over time, but starts with these basic decisions.

Signals

There are two types of signals that an umpire will use. One set is used to communicate to the players, coaches, scorers and spectators the decisions made during the game, e.g. safe and out, fair and foul.

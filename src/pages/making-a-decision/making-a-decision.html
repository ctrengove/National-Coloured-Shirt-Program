Making A Decision

The leading Edge

This is the side of the base in which the base runner is approaching. In order to determine whether a base runner has beaten a throw or tag, the leading edge provides the clearest view.

Positioning

You need to keep the play in front of you so that you can see

  • The ball

  • The fielder making the play

  • The base runner (or batter-runner)

  • The base (or area) where the play will happen

With these four essential elements in view let the play happen, then make your call, don't anticipate the result.

Remember that nothing can happen without the ball, so maintain an awareness of where the ball is.

Safe or Out

The batter is out when…

  • the third strike is swung at, and missed, and is caught by the catcher

  • the batter watches the third strike cross the plate and is caught by the catcher

The batter-runner is out when…

  • a fielder catches a fly ball on the full

  • hitting a fair ball, they are tagged with the ball in the glove or bare hand while off base, or thrown out (by a fielder touching the base while holding the ball) prior to reaching 1st base

    Note: A legal tag requires the fielder to have the ball securely held in hand or glove when tagging the runner

A base runner is out when…

  • on a force play (ie, when the runner is forced to advance because the batter becomes a batterrunner), a fielder holding the ball contacts the base to which the runner is forced to advance

  • the ball is in play and the runner is legally tagged by a fielder when the runner is off the base

Batters, batter-runners and base runners are safe when…

  • the above do not happen

  • the ball is not caught

  • a runner beats the ball to the base
